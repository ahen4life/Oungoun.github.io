Scrap my car park!

Scrap the Internet, earn money

Posted by Ben on August 24, 2017

Where, where????

A few time ago I'm getting ready for a job in Metz Football Stadium.

Today, I have found what corresponds to my investment in terms of initial investment -not so much-yield and risk exposure. It will be probabily be one or two cars parks.

 

But where ...?

 

Indeed, two points seem to me:

1. Not invest too much while earning A LOT of money

2. Be able to sell my car park if I want to (Bitcoin lovers, think about it)

 

So obviously, I spent hours on the internet reading newspapers and specialized articles. I have a good idea of ​​the important parameters and I will tell you something, I still want to do something dealing with scraping and data vizualization. So let's do this.

 

So remember, I am only making, for now, a static analyse.

 

Let's go back to my two points.

 

On the one hand, it seems to be good to have a look at the ratio between rent and purchase prices. Indeed, I do not want to loose money just because I bought something in Neuilly-sur-Seine instead of Bagneux.

 

On the other hand, Be able to sell my park if I want to, I will just compare how many parks are bought a year.

 

 

And yes, do not forget this point, generally speaking, the more you buy, the cheaper. Ask your solicitor.

 

 

The data is coming from a well-known sell and buy french platform. We take on this first observation data from 2017, October 23 rd to 2017, December 23rd.
