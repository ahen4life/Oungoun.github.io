So here we are.

As I told you, back in the main page, I am a consultant based in Paris.
Over the past year, at Kyu Associés, I was lucky to spend 3 months in a brand new 4.0 plant in Columbus, Indiana. There, I worked as a Supply Chain Manager and was in charge of setting up and managing the local Supply Chain.

Back in France, I spent 3 more months fully understanding the issues that our costumer was facing and finally decided to write a research article, helped by members of Kyu Associés and Arts et Métiers ParisTech Engineering University. You can learn more about this article and even download it using this link.

Then, still working at Kyu in Paris, I decided to start learning more about optimization processes and studied this really nice book, written by S. Tamayo, R. Cardin, S. Echeverri and D. Pino : Recherche opérationnelle appliquée à la gestion industrielle (Operational reaserch applied to industrial management) . What I really liked in this book, is that 4 well-known french researchers decided to do something really operational. Indeed, during the last year I read a lot of research articles but none of them was as clear when it came to… reality.

Here is the point. While making some research on the internet and improving my R skills, I found this this article . A young graduate, decided to use his skills on R, higlighted by the ShinyApp interface, to share a user-friendly and smart optimization tool. I would like to thanks this man, who probably does not know me, because he gave me the envy to do something ;)

So I am still working on something similar but it takes a lot of time and...I am working on it. Moreover, I am trying to do and then "to improve" what he did... By the way, to share with you the power of R / Shinyapp, I decided, initially, to write this article : Scrap my car park! using the power of data scraping & visualization to convince my banker to give me a loan.

Finally, I am somewhere between research and operations. I love to learn, mostly, I love to use what I learned.

For now, I am looking for an opportunity abroad (outside of France) dealing with Supply Chain, Optimization & Data Science.
